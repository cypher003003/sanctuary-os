Classified Intel: Project Sanctuary

3-DAY OPERATIONAL SCHEDULE

📍 The Aditya Birla Public School, Hirmi  |  🛡 Commanded by: Robust Data Developers

DAY 1: Micro-Architecture & Embedded Security
🎯 Objective: Hardware initialization and establishing secure physical baselines.
  • 0900 Hours

    Mission Briefing

    Welcome to Project: Sanctuary. Introduction to the threat landscape and SOC (Security Operations Center) protocols.

  • 1030 Hours

    Hardware Deployment

    Distribution of IoT components and microcontrollers.

  • 1130 Hours

    System Calibration

    Mandatory workstation setup. Operatives will install necessary CH340 / CP210x USB-to-UART bridge drivers.

  • 1330 Hours

    Embedded Security

    Understanding vulnerabilities in hardware architecture and writing secure base-level code.

  • 1500 Hours

    Day 1 Debrief

    Hardware lockdown and secure storage.

DAY 2: Network Topologies & Data Telemetry
🎯 Objective: Securing communications, understanding data flow, and threat modeling.
  • 0900 Hours

    Network Reconnaissance

    Introduction to local and wide-area network structures.

  • 1030 Hours

    Telemetry & Interception

    How data moves across IoT devices, packet analysis, and identifying man-in-the-middle vulnerabilities.

  • 1330 Hours

    Cryptographic Protocols

    Securing data streams and understanding encryption standards.

  • 1430 Hours

    The Divide (Team Assignments)

    Using the live Command Center dashboard, instructors officially divide recruits into their Day 3 operational units:

    🔴 VRU

    Vanguard Recon Unit
    Offensive Operations

    🔵 SIS

    Sanctuary Intelligence Service
    Defensive Operations

  • 1500 Hours

    Day 2 Debrief

    Teams separate to strategize for the final engagement.

DAY 3: Operation: Aegis-Vanguard
🎯 Objective: Full-scale live simulation testing offensive and defensive capabilities.
  • 0900 Hours

    Rules of Engagement

    Final briefing on the cyber-range environment and scoring parameters.

  • 0930 Hours

    Operation: Aegis-Vanguard Commences

    🔴 VRU (Offensive)

    Attempt to breach the IoT network, intercept telemetry data, and compromise the simulated grid.

    🔵 SIS (Defensive)

    Monitor the SOC dashboard, detect intrusions, patch vulnerabilities on the fly, and maintain system uptime.

  • 1300 Hours

    Ceasefire & Data Recovery

    Operations halt. Systems are frozen for analysis.

  • 1400 Hours

    Mission Aftermath

    Joint debriefing. Instructors walk through the logs to show how breaches occurred and how they were defended.

  • 1530 Hours

    Honorable Discharge

    Final closing remarks and certificate distribution by Robust Data Developers.
